Troubleshoot

Issue: Uploaded a new file, but all other devices download the previous file instead of the latest one

Solution : This typically occurs due to caching. You can simply go to your browser settings and clear cached images and files, to resolve the issue.

Issue: Send file from one device, but download option not showing on receiver or other devices

Try refreshing your web page; most of the time, that solves the issue.

Issue: I am entering the connection url in another pc but its not loading the webpage

Check the connection url you are entering is correct,and also make sure your devices are connected on same network.
